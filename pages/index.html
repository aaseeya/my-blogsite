How to prepare simple noodles recipe

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • onion
  • green and red pepper
  • oil
  • seasoning and spices

Preparations:

Slice your onion and pepper and keep aside. boil 1 and half cup of water that is if your are going to cook pack of 120kg of noodles and you like it to be juicy and if you prefer it korean style that is extra juicy lol...then use 2 cups of water, but if you are the type that like your noodles to be dry then use 1 cup of water. Add your noodles into the boiled water and at thesame time add your sliced onion, pepper and 1 tbl spoon of oil with seasonig and spices of your choice, let it cook for 5mins and your noodles is ready.
